Greenville County Fair Coming Soon

    Why YOU should go to the fair!

  • The food! - All the food you can think of, and the best part is that 90% of it is on a stick!
  • The rides! - Whether you are old or young you can find the ride for you. Your kids will love you for it!
  • The animals - Greenville will give you an experience you will never forget with animals you may only be able to see at the zoo.
  • The entertainment - You might even be surprised to see your next door neighbor's kids performing at your local fair.
  • The education - You can learn a lot about your local town's history by just attending the fair.
  • The art - Submit your own art, or view the art of others from around the community. This is a great place to see hidden talents revealed.
  • The competitions - Local competitions give you the chance to stand toe to toe against or shoulder to shoulder with your neighbors to compete for those cool blue ribbons.
